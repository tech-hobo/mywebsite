About Us

Every journey starts somewhere. Tech Hobo Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting technology education and bridging the digital divide. We provide resources, mentorship, and opportunities to children from underserved communities to explore and learn about technology.

Our foundation’s mission is to inspire the next generation of innovators, problem solvers, and creators by offering access to technology and supporting hands-on learning experiences.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make technology accessible to all children, regardless of background, and inspire them to become the innovators and creators of tomorrow.

  • Promote equal access to technology
  • Empower the youth with tech skills
  • Inspire creativity and problem-solving through tech

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child has the opportunity to learn and grow in the ever-evolving digital landscape. Our goal is to bridge the digital divide and provide a pathway for children from underserved communities to explore and thrive in the world of technology.
