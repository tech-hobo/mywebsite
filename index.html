Welcome to Tech Hobo Foundation

Every journey starts somewhere. This site is dedicated to capturing the stories, lessons, and connections that make technology more than just machines.

Our Mission

The TechHobo Foundation is a nonprofit organization founded by David Knapp with the mission of inspiring and educating children about technology. We aim to provide resources, opportunities, and hands-on experiences to spark excitement about STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) fields, particularly in underserved communities.

Our Vision

The foundation's vision includes:

  • Organizing workshops, programs, or events to teach kids about coding, robotics, and other tech skills.
  • Sharing stories, lessons, and insights from working in IT across various industries through its blog and online presence.
  • Encouraging creativity and problem-solving in young minds to help them see technology as a tool for innovation and empowerment.

Our ultimate goal is to make technology accessible and exciting for kids while fostering a future generation of tech enthusiasts and innovators.
