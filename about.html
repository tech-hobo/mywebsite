Our Story

Founded with a vision to bridge the technology gap for children, TechHobo Foundation provides free education, tools, and resources to empower the next generation of tech leaders. Through our coding bootcamps, STEM workshops, and collaborative projects, we strive to make technology accessible and engaging for kids from all backgrounds.
